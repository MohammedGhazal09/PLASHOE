<objective>
Create authenticated backend wishlist persistence and protected list/add/remove APIs with validation and focused route tests.
</objective>

## Context

- No backend wishlist model or route exists today.
- Cart is the closest authenticated persistence pattern.
- Phase 14 must not weaken auth, cart, checkout, or stock behavior.

## Recommendation

Start with backend persistence so frontend authenticated sync has a stable contract. Use the cart route pattern, but keep wishlist items product-only rather than cart line items.
