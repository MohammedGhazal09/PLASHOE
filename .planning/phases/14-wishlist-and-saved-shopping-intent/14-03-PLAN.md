<objective>
Expose wishlist state consistently across product browsing, Quick View, Header, and Account, including move-to-cart without losing saved intent on cart failure.
</objective>

## Context

- `14-UI-SPEC.md` locks compact, commerce-native wishlist surfaces.
- ProductCard and QuickView already have add-to-cart behavior and selected-size defaults.
- Account already has a Wishlist tab placeholder and auth form.

## Recommendation

Add the reusable WishlistButton first, then wire it into each surface. Keep Account as the management surface for remove and move-to-cart.
