# Phase 21 Discussion Log

## Recommendation
Proceed without asking for more product decisions because the roadmap already defines the feature boundary. The safest default is a provider-free admin-maintained lookbook with product-reference validation and cart-store-based add-to-cart behavior.

## Decisions
- Dedicated lookbook entries will contain scene metadata, hotspots, and optional bundle item lists.
- Products remain authoritative for price, stock, image, sizes, and cart behavior.
- Public shoppers see active entries only; admins can manage draft and active entries.
- Bundle pricing discounts are deferred to future coupon or promotion work.

## Open Questions
- Future merchandising may need scheduled publishing, curated audience segments, or bundle-specific discounts. Those are intentionally left out of this phase because they need clearer operations and pricing rules.
