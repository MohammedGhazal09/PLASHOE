# Plan 15-03 - Product Detail Page, Reviews UI, Fit Guidance, and Related Products

<objective>
Build the shopper-facing product detail page using the backend contracts from Plans 15-01 and 15-02.
</objective>

## Tasks

1. Add `reviewsApi` wrapper for list/create review endpoints.
2. Add `ProductDetail.jsx` with product loading, size selection, wishlist, add-to-cart, materials/care, fit guidance, reviews, review form, and related products.
3. Wire `/products/:id` into `App.js` and `pages/index.js`.
4. Enable reviews by default in frontend config with exact `false` kill switch.
5. Add tests for product loading, route rendering, add-to-cart, wishlist presence, review list, review submission gates, fit guidance, related products, and config default.
6. Preserve local fallback/error behavior for invalid or unavailable product ids.
