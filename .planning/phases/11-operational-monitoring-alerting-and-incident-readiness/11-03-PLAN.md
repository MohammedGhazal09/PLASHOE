<objective>
Document MongoDB backup/restore readiness, create the consolidated incident and rollback runbook, and produce the final Phase 11 verification ledger.
</objective>

## Context

Plans 11-01 and 11-02 create the local operational signals and durable operations catalog. This plan completes MON-03 and MON-04 by documenting restore procedure, blocked restore drill evidence, incident response, rollback, and final verification.

## Recommendation

Treat actual restore drills and host-specific rollback commands as required-before-Phase-12 when provider access is missing. The phase can still complete its source-controlled readiness contract if the blockers are explicit.

## Artifacts This Plan Produces

- MongoDB backup and restore sections in `docs/OPERATIONS.md`.
- `docs/INCIDENT-RESPONSE.md`.
- `.planning/phases/11-operational-monitoring-alerting-and-incident-readiness/11-VERIFICATION.md`.
- Finalized `.planning/phases/11-operational-monitoring-alerting-and-incident-readiness/11-OPERATIONS-EVIDENCE.md` rows for backup, runbook, and blocked live proof.

<threat_model>

### T-11-07: Unsafe Restore Procedure

Risk: restore instructions target production data or cause secret-bearing backup artifacts to enter source control.

Mitigation: require provider-managed backups, disposable staging restore target, redacted evidence, and explicit ban on committed connection strings, backup exports, account IDs, or screenshots with secrets.

### T-11-08: Incident Runbook Is Not Executable

Risk: the runbook is generic and does not tell operators what to check in the first 5/15/60 minutes.

Mitigation: reuse `docs/DEPLOYMENT.md` smoke checks and add scenario-specific symptoms, triage, decision thresholds, rollback triggers, verification, communication cadence, and escalation.

### T-11-09: Verification Marks Blocked Live Evidence as Passed

Risk: Phase 11 hides Phase 9 blockers by calling docs complete enough.

Mitigation: final `11-VERIFICATION.md` must separate local/source-control evidence from live-provider evidence and leave unavailable provider rows blocked.

</threat_model>
